as you walk along the battlements of your castle, you watch as a great fire
burns down a large portion of the surrounding countryside. you make out a 
dark mass emerging from the inferno, and you give out a nervous order to 
your knights, "protect the women and children with your lives, i will face 
him myself." your knights give a slight bow and rush to the aid of the 
peasants inside the castle walls. as you make your way down to the castle 
gates and down the grassy hill on which your mighty castle is perched, you 
begin to make out the bright armor of your assailant and on his sheild, the 
daunting crest of house payne.
